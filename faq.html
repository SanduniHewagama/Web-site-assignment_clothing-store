FAQs

What is your return policy?

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you're not happy with your order, you can return it within 30 days of receiving it for a full refund or exchange. Just make sure the items are unworn, unwashed, and in their original condition with tags attached. For more information, please visit our Returns & Exchanges page.

How long will it take to receive my order?

Once your order is placed, it typically takes 1-2 business days for us to process and ship it. Shipping times vary depending on your location and the shipping method chosen at checkout. You can track your order's progress using the tracking number provided in your shipping confirmation email.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide! International shipping rates and delivery times may vary depending on your location and the shipping method selected. Please note that customers are responsible for any customs duties or taxes imposed by their country's customs office.

Do you restock items that are out of stock?

We do our best to restock popular items whenever possible. However, some items may be limited edition or seasonal and may not be restocked once they're sold out. To stay updated on restocks and new arrivals, we recommend signing up for our newsletter or following us on social media.

How can I contact customer support if I have a question or issue?

We're here to help! You can reach our customer support team via email at support@bella.com or through our online contact form. Our support hours are Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM (EST). We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours. Additionally, you can check out our FAQ page for quick answers to common questions.
